Franck Bohbot

Capturing the haze

Franck Bohbot is a french photographer and filmmaker based in Brooklyn, NY and Los Angeles, CA, who explores the extraordinary of everyday life. His dreamlike style, combined with his use of color carefully constructed compositions, elevated ordinary subjects to the level of art, leading the viewer's eye in the same direction as the subjects are looking.
